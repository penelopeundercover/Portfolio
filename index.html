About Me

Becoming a web developer! I'm currently a student in a web development bootcamp. My current profession: dozer hand. I enjoy learning how things work, and how to make them work. It feels like I'm leaving one kind of construction and entering another. Different tools, different materials, but the same underlying mindset: make it work. When I first started construction, I was very booksmart but had no common sense whatsoever. I was challenged to use a whole different part of my mind--to think in a completely different way than ever before. And now I'm a Lead Operator. It's time to leave my comfort zone and challenge my mind yet again. I'm excited to see what contributions I can make to this new field.

I am known as a dependable and positive person. I'm also a person who stays calm in crisis, while still performing with an appropriate sense of urgency. I live in a pretty little farming town in southeast Kansas called Fredonia with my husband and dog. I've been playing the piano since I was 8, have an unimpressive but helpful daily yoga routine, and enjoy reading the works of Jane Austen, John Donne, and A.W.Tozer. I also enjoy cooking, doing artsy projects with items I find in nature, and learning to do minor car repairs with the guidance of youtube.
